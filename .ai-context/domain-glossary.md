# Domain Glossary

This glossary defines key terms used across specs and implementation files.

## SEO and Content

- Programmatic SEO: Producing many high-value pages from a repeatable template and keyword system.
- Search intent: Why a user searches (`informational`, `comparison`, `tutorial`).
- SERP: Search Engine Results Page.
- CTR: Click-through rate from impressions to clicks.
- E-E-A-T: Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness.

## Content Pipeline

- Quality gate: Mandatory checks before an article can be published.
- Rewrite mode: Regeneration flow for low-performing pages flagged by GSC data.
- Frontmatter: Structured metadata block at the top of each MDX article.

## Monetisation

- Affiliate CTA: Link block that routes users to a partner offer.
- CTA variant: A/B test version of CTA copy, position, or format.
- Revenue MTD: Month-to-date estimated affiliate revenue.

## Video Pipeline

- Script extraction: Converting article sections into narration-ready script segments.
- Longform video: Full-length video output aligned with article structure.
- Shorts: Short-form vertical clips distilled from article highlights.
